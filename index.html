Fundamentals

The Basics

Three things every plant needs to survive and thrive.

💧

Watering

Most plants prefer to dry out slightly between waterings. Check the soil with your finger — if the top inch is dry, it's time to water. Overwatering is the #1 cause of plant death.

☀️

Light

Match your plant to your light conditions. Succulents love bright direct sun; ferns and pothos thrive in low indirect light. A south-facing window is the brightest spot in your home.

🌿

Soil

Use the right soil mix for your plant type. Cacti need fast-draining gritty mix; tropical plants prefer rich, moisture-retaining potting mix. Good drainage prevents root rot.

Advanced Care

Care Tips

Level up your plant care with these simple habits.

📅

Seasonal Adjustments

Plants grow slower in winter — reduce watering and skip fertilizing until spring. Increase light exposure as days get shorter.

🌳

Repotting

Repot when you see roots escaping the drainage holes or growth slowing down. Go up one pot size at a time.

💦

Humidity

Tropical plants love humidity. Group plants together or place a tray of pebbles and water underneath.

🪴

Fertilizing

Feed during the growing season (spring and summer) with a balanced liquid fertilizer every 2–4 weeks.
